Action Options

#Whiff-Punish

  • Baiting out an attack, then punishing the end-lag
  • Beats #Attacking

#Taking-Space

When an opponent is attempting to #whiff-punish you, you can simply continue to move forward, gaining more #Stage-Space to use later.

  • Beats #Whiff-Punish

#Reading

When you know what the opponent will do, and act so that your option comes out early, before their reaction time can complete their intended action.

Resources

#Stage-Space

The stage space behind you is area that you can spend to add space between you , allowing for you #Whiff-Punish

Action Risk Reward
Taking Space Get hit Gain space
Attacking Get hit Land hit
Whiff Punishing lost space Land hit

Commitments

#Hard
  • Immediately punishable
  • Able to be punished on reaction
  • High risk, high reward
  • Attacks with long end-lag are assumed hard
  • Recognize what happened before
#Soft
  • Not immediately punishable
  • Must be use #Reading
  • Attacks with short end-lag are assumed soft
  • Watch what happens after
    • This is the action that you should be punishing

Determining the commitment level

  • Spacing: The closer you are, the harder your commitments become
  • Move end-lag

Attack Spacing

Attack spacing can be broken into 3 parts

#Over-Shooting

When you attack in the space beyond where an opponent currently is

  • #Hard Commit
  • Is a form of #Taking-Space
  • Beats #Whiff-Punish

#Under-Shooting

When you are #Reading where you expect opponent to be

  • Beats #Over-Shooting

#Whiff-Punish

  • Beats #Under-Shooting
